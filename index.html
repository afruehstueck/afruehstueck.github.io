I'm a recent PhD graduate from the Visual Computing Center at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology working with Prof. Peter Wonka.

In Summer 2023, I will be joining Adobe UK as a Research Scientist.

I'm broadly interested in computer graphics, especially image synthesis and generative modeling techniques. My research involves the generation of large-scale images using deep neural networks, focusing on the quality of the generated content, controllability and the plausible compositing of the output.
